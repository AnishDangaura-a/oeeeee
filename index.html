Two Decades of Excellence

2001

Aurum Grand opens its doors with 45 rooms, a single restaurant, and a singular vision: to create the world's most personal luxury experience.

Questions

Frequently Asked

What is your cancellation policy?
+
Reservations may be cancelled without charge up to 48 hours prior to arrival. Within 48 hours, a one-night charge applies. Presidential Suite requires 7 days' notice.
Is early check-in available?
+
Standard check-in is from 3pm. Early check-in from 12pm may be arranged subject to availability at a supplementary charge. Luggage storage is always complimentary.
Are pets welcome?
+
We warmly welcome well-behaved dogs up to 10kg in select rooms. Our concierge can arrange gourmet pet meals, walking services, and premium amenities.
Do you offer airport transfers?
+
Complimentary luxury transfers are included with Grand Suite and Presidential Suite bookings. Private transfers for other rooms can be arranged at preferential rates.
What is the dress code at MIRO?
+
MIRO maintains a smart-elegant dress code. No sportswear or beachwear please. TERRA and NOIR welcome smart-casual attire. Our concierge is happy to advise.
